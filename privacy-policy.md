# BankStreet Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 12, 2026**

BankStreet ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by Arunkumar Velusamy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the BankStreet mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive:
- Your email address
- Your display name
- A unique user identifier from the authentication provider

### Portfolio Data
You may voluntarily provide financial portfolio data including:
- Stock holdings, unit trusts, and other investment positions
- Subscription and recurring payment information
- Credit card information (last 4 digits only — we never store full card numbers)
- Bank account labels and balances you choose to enter

### AI Chat History
- Messages you send to the AI assistant
- Images you upload for analysis
- Daily usage counts for rate limiting

### Subscription Information
If you subscribe to BankStreet Pro, we receive:
- Subscription receipt from Apple App Store
- Subscription status and expiry date
- We do NOT receive your payment method or billing details — these are handled by Apple

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide portfolio tracking and analysis features
- To send your messages to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered responses
- To enforce daily usage limits on the free tier
- To verify subscription status and unlock Pro features
- To authenticate you on subsequent app launches

## Third-Party Services

We share data with the following services to provide app functionality:
- **Firebase Authentication** (Google) — for sign-in
- **Anthropic Claude API** — for AI chat responses (your messages are sent to Anthropic)
- **Render.com** — backend hosting
- **Apple App Store** — for subscription processing

We do NOT sell your data, share it for advertising, or use it for any purpose other than providing the app's features.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers operated by Render.com in Singapore. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us
- Cancel your Pro subscription anytime via iOS Settings → Subscriptions
- Stop using the app at any time

## Children's Privacy

BankStreet is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
